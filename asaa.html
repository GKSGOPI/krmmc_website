- Function of the Placement cell

The college has a very effective Placement Cell that has been catering to the needs of students by enabling them to get placed in reputed organizations and providing updated information on career opportunities.


Some of the functions of the cell are:


  • Enabling Students to get placed in top-notch organizations through campus recruitment.
  • Extending information to students on job opportunities and helping them to get placed through off- campus interviews
  • Organizing Pre Placement Training Programmes to improve the employability skills of the students.
  • Focusing on overall grooming and personality development of the students.
  • Conducting career development seminars to create awareness about the importance of higher studies from diploma to professional courses in India and Abroad.
  • Helping students to get part time jobs and arranging for Internship programmes while pursuing their career.
  • Extending information on emerging professional trends, events, job profiles, career opportunities in different fields of study.
  • Facilitating the recruitment procedure.
  • Creating linkages with Industries and Institutions
  • Organising Job Fair in association with Industry
